How to change PWA Icons

To change the PWA (Progressive Web App) icon located in the public > pwa-icon directory in your project, follow these steps:

  • Prepare your new icon: You'll need a new icon in the desired format and dimensions. The icon should be a square PNG image with a transparent background
  • Replace the existing icon: Navigate to the public > pwa-icon directory in your your project, and replace the existing icon-00x00.png file with your new icon.
  • Clear the cache: If you've previously visited your PWA, your browser may have cached the old icon. To ensure the new icon is displayed, clear your browser cache and reload the PWA.

That's it! Your PWA icon should now be updated with your new icon.
